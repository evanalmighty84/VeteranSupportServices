Who we Are

About Veterans Support Services:

Veterans Support Services was founded on the foundation of being a Veteran owned, principal-based, specialized business engineering company and is staffed as such. Since inception, everyone wishing to join the team must have uncompromising honesty, integrity, and character among their core values. Everyone within our organization embraces living up to these high standards in all aspects of their lives. These are never gray areas.

Veterans Support Services understands that we must put our success second to that of our clients. We pride ourselves on excellence, and would not have it any other way. Our guarantee reminds everyone at Veterans Support Services that we must always remain steadfast in discipline, focus, and ensuring that our clients will receive what we promise and what they expect.

Culture:

It's not always easy going above and beyond for our clients, but it's paramount for Veterans Support Services. We're partners in the trust, hope, and the dreams of our clients. The responsibilities we bear and sacrifices we make is worth the quality of life we give to our clients, their employees, and their families.

 

Veteran Owned:

We understand that freedom isn't free because we have served to ensure that our freedoms are not compromised. We believe in this country, our constitution and the values that make it great. More to the point, we believe in these truths because we have lived and experienced them firsthand.

 

Our Guarantee:

We stand behind our work. Our guarantee is iron-clad. If we do not deliver what we promise, you owe us nothing.

No small print. No caveats. Just our commitment to deliver what we promise so that we meet or exceed your expectations.
